Why Forge
Hardware design, reimagined
No schematics. No CAD. No boilerplate. Just describe what you need.
⚡

6 build backends

OpenSCAD, CadQuery, Zoo Text-to-CAD, LLaMA-Mesh, Hunyuan3D, Cosmos — auto-selected or choose your own.

✔

Auto-validated

6 design rule checks catch pin conflicts, power issues, and wiring errors before you build.

◾

PCB + 3D print ready

SKiDL netlists for KiCad, parametric enclosures with snap-fit, cutouts, mounts, and ventilation.

⚙

250+ product archetypes

Say "pulse oximeter" or "smart doorbell" — Forge knows the components. 30+ medical archetypes, WHO + CE regulatory docs, IP-rated enclosures.

📱

Offline-first, battery-ready

Auto-generates low-power firmware, sleep modes, and watchdog timers. Designed for devices that run for months on batteries.

⚗

MCP native

Use from Claude, GPT, Gemini, or any MCP client. Hardware design as a conversation.
